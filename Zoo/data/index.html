Welcome to the Animal World

Explore the fascinating world of animals. Learn about different species, their habitats, and behaviors. Discover the beauty and diversity of wildlife.

Animals are multicellular, eukaryotic organisms that form the biological kingdom Animalia. With over a million known species, they play a crucial role in our ecosystem. From the mighty elephant to the tiny ant, every animal has its own unique characteristics and importance.
