Pantheon

The Norse pantheon consists of various gods, goddesses, and other mythical beings. Some of the most prominent gods include Odin, the chief god associated with wisdom, war, and poetry; Thor, the thunder god known for his strength and bravery; Loki, the trickster god associated with mischief and chaos; Freyja, the goddess of love, beauty, and fertility; and Freyr, the god of prosperity, fertility, and sunshine.
